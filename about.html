这是一个以生命树为主题的泡泡墙作品。由多名创客共同创作完成。通过背景图，讲述一个关于生命树的传说故事（每天中午，树叶一片一片地变成泡泡，升到空中，带给人们快乐 ... ...），以表述对孩子的爱，和对生命的敬意。

拍摄于，2015年壹零科技节，泡泡墙展示现场。China Beijing 2015-10-03

泡泡墙，高2.8米，宽5米，由40只泡泡枪以阵列形式，分布在墙体的中心区域。整个系统，采用了自动补充泡泡液，集中供电、集中控制的方案。一次补液后，在无人干预的情况下，可以连续工作6-8小时。

由于控制系统采用了总线结构，可以方便地扩展各种交互式体验。本项目，实现了面对“生命树”大声呼喊，触发泡泡墙吐出很多的泡泡。有趣，试试吧！ ：）

团队成员：littleJames, Peter Pan, 小飞鱼, 无敌的蛋壳(bigEgg) ... ...

==== English Version ====

This is the bubble wall taking "Lift Tree" as the theme, which is made by several makers. It is telling a legend about the tree throughthe background picture (Everyday at noon, tree's leaves become bubbles, rising into the sky and bring happiness to people ...), to express love for kids and respect for life.

The bubble wall is 2.8 meters high and 5 meters wide. There are 40 bubble guns being distributed in the central area with array configuration. The whole system adopts a scheme which can supply bubble liquid automatically, provide power intensively and control intensively. It could work 6-8 hours without anyone's help after replenishing the solution.

The bus structure of control system makes it easier to extend the number of bubble guns and sensors. In this way, It could create a variety of interactive expresences. When you face to the life tree, yelling and touching, it will spit out many bubbles. We can try it. :)
